# The Sword Mountains

_The rugged mountain range that rises southeast of [[Phandalin]] and runs north toward [[Neverwinter]]. Riddled with old dwarven mines, orc lairs, ancient ruins, and the occasional wyvern. The geographic backdrop of nearly every quest in this campaign's first arc._

The Sword Mountains are not the highest range in [[Faerûn]] but they are some of the most thoroughly _used_ — by dwarves who mined them for a thousand years, by orcs who raid down from them today, by hidden Netherese ruins, and by the legendary dwarven complex of [[Wave Echo Cave]] hidden somewhere in their foothills.

**Things in this campaign that lie in or near the Sword Mountains:**
- [[Wave Echo Cave]] — somewhere in the western foothills.
- [[Wyvern Tor]] — a rocky crag on the western edge.
- [[Old Owl Well]] — Netherese ruin in the foothills northeast of [[Phandalin]].
- **Cragmaw goblin holdings** — including [[Cragmaw Castle]] in the [[Neverwinter Wood]] just north of the range.
- **Orc tribes** — periodically raiding southward, occasionally down to Phandalin.
