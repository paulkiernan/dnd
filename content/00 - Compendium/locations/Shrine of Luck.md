# The Shrine of Luck

_A small stone shrine to [[forgotten-realms-tymora|Tymora]], goddess of luck and adventurers. Tended by [[Sister Garaele]]. The spiritual heart of [[Phandalin]] — such as it is._

The shrine sits on the south side of [[Phandalin]], between the [[Miner's Exchange]] and a row of small craftsmen's cottages. It is little more than a single room of carved stone: a statue of Tymora in the center (a woman in motion, coin in hand, smiling), a row of low wooden benches, and a brass offering bowl that is rarely full and never quite empty.

Townsfolk drop in to ask for a coin's blessing before any risky undertaking — a mine descent, a long ride, a wedding. [[Sister Garaele|Garaele]] is happy to provide one.

A small back room serves as Garaele's living quarters: a cot, a writing desk, a small shrine to Tymora more personal than the public one, and a locked chest containing — among other Harper paraphernalia — a silver harp pin.

**Resident:** [[Sister Garaele]].
