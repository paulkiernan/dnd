# Cragmaw Castle

_The ruined castle in the [[Neverwinter Wood]] that serves as the stronghold of the **Cragmaw goblin tribe**. Current location of the captured [[Gundren Rockseeker]]._

---

## Overview

Cragmaw Castle was, centuries ago, a small frontier fortification built by an old human noble house and abandoned when the family failed out. Today it is half a ruin — collapsed towers, a broken outer wall, a partially-intact great hall — and entirely the property of the Cragmaw tribe, who have unified several local goblin warbands under a hobgoblin warlord called **King Grol**.

The castle sits deep in the [[Neverwinter Wood]], somewhere northeast of [[Phandalin]]. **The party does not yet know the exact location.** [[Sildar Hallwinter]] is working on it. Likely ways to learn:

1. Capture a Cragmaw scout — preferably from a hunting party encountered on the road.
2. Find correspondence at [[Tresendar Manor]] that references the castle.

---

## Why The Party Will Go

- To rescue [[Gundren Rockseeker]] (their original employer; the entire campaign began escorting his wagon).
- To recover the **map to [[Wave Echo Cave]]** that Gundren was carrying when he was captured.
- To learn who is _paying_ the Cragmaw goblins to do this — and why.
