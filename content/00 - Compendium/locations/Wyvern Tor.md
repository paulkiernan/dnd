# Wyvern Tor

_A prominent rocky crag in the hills east of [[Phandalin]], named for the wyverns that used to nest on its peak. Currently the camp of a small **orc raiding party** that has been hitting caravans on the [[Triboar Trail]] and harassing farms along the eastern road._

---

## Overview

Wyvern Tor is a single tall stone outcropping rising from a stretch of rough hill country roughly a day's ride east of [[Phandalin]]. The summit is bare rock, the slopes are scrub and sparse pine, and a series of natural caves in the southern face have been used historically by every kind of large creature looking for a defensible roost: bears, wyverns, ogres, and — most recently — orcs.

The wyverns the tor was named for have not been seen for two generations. The orcs moved in roughly six months ago.

---

## The Current Threat

The orc band at Wyvern Tor has been:
- Raiding caravans on the [[Triboar Trail]].
- Butchering livestock from outlying farms.
- Intimidating travelers in the eastern hills.

They have not yet attacked [[Phandalin]] itself. Local opinion in town is divided between "they will, eventually" and "they're getting fewer the longer the winter goes on." Nobody has actually been up to count them.

---

## The Bounty

[[Harbin Wester]] has posted a standing town bounty: **100gp** for clearing the orc presence at Wyvern Tor. The bounty has been posted for nearly two months without takers.

[[Daran Edermath]] privately considers 100gp insultingly low for the work and will tell the party so. He suggests negotiating Harbin up.
