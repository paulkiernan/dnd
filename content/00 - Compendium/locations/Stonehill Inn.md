# Stonehill Inn

_The only proper inn in [[Phandalin]]. Run by [[Toblen Stonehill]] and his family. The social hub of the town._

A modest two-story timber-and-stone building on the north side of the town square. Six rooms upstairs (the sixth currently occupied by [[Sildar Hallwinter]]). A warm common room downstairs that serves as the town's living room — locals come for ale, gossip, and Trilena Stonehill's stew, which is the best in fifty miles.

Rooms: **5sp/night**, meals included.

If you want to know what's happening in [[Phandalin]], you sit at the Stonehill bar for an evening. You will learn everything that matters and nothing that the [[The Redbrands|Redbrands]] do not want known.

**Staff:** [[Toblen Stonehill]] (innkeeper), **Trilena Stonehill** (cook & spouse), **Elsa** (dwarf bartender, sharp-tongued).

**Notable patrons:** [[Sildar Hallwinter]] (currently resident), [[Daran Edermath]] (visits for cider).
