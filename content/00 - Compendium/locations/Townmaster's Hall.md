# The Townmaster's Hall

_A sturdy two-room stone building on the north side of [[Phandalin]]'s town square. The closest thing the town has to a seat of government and a courthouse. Currently occupied by townmaster [[Harbin Wester]]._

The front room is the public hall: a desk for the townmaster, a low railing dividing the room, and a wall covered in the town **job board** — a cork wall plastered with bounty notices, missing-person flyers, mining-claim disputes, and the occasional commercial advertisement.

The back room is the **jail** — two small cells with iron-bound doors. They are well-built and rarely used. The lock is good; the man with the key is [[Harbin Wester]].

The hall's current standing job notice: _"Bounty: 100gp for clearing orc raiders out of [[Wyvern Tor]]. Apply within."_

**Resident:** [[Harbin Wester]].
