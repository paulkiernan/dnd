# Neverwinter Wood

_The vast ancient forest sprawling north and northeast of [[Neverwinter]] and east of [[Phandalin]]. Beautiful, dangerous, ancient. Home to druids, fey creatures, and darker things._

The Neverwinter Wood covers a huge stretch of the western [[The Sword Coast|Sword Coast]] interior. It is old — older than most of the human settlements at its edge. Travelers say no two people who go in come out by the same path.

Sometimes referred to in older texts as "the Neverwinter Wood" or simply "the Wood." [[Triboar Trail]] skirts its southern edge.

**Known features within or beyond the Wood:**
- [[Cragmaw Castle]] — somewhere deep in the woods, current stronghold of the Cragmaw goblin tribe.
- The ruined village of **Thundertree** — north of the wood, destroyed long ago by the eruption of Mount Hotenow.
- Old druid circles and standing stones — most known only to the druids who still walk among them.
