# The Lords' Alliance

_A coalition of cities and city-states along the [[The Sword Coast|Sword Coast]] and the North that work together for mutual security and prosperity. Includes [[Neverwinter]], [[Waterdeep]], Silverymoon, Mirabar, Daggerford, and others._

---

## In Brief

The Lords' Alliance is the closest thing the Sword Coast has to a "government" — a loose alliance of major cities, each represented by a noble or appointed official. The Alliance is concerned with **threats to civilization**: bandit lords, monstrous incursions, hostile factions like the [[The Zhentarim|Zhentarim]], and the slow drift of frontier settlements into lawlessness.

They operate through a mix of diplomacy, mercenary contracts, and quietly-placed agents. They are not subtle — Alliance agents tend to wear their colors openly, carry papers of marque, and expect cooperation from local authorities.

## Reputation

The Alliance is **respected** in [[Neverwinter]] and [[Waterdeep]], **mistrusted** in places like Luskan (which considers them imperialist), and **mostly unknown** in small frontier towns like [[Phandalin]] where their reach is, frankly, theoretical.
