# Sister Garaele — Cleric of Tymora

_Earnest young elf, acolyte of [[forgotten-realms-tymora|Tymora]] at the [[Shrine of Luck]] in [[Phandalin]]._

---

## At a Glance

- **Race / Class:** Elf, Cleric of [[forgotten-realms-tymora|Tymora]] (Lady Luck).
- **Where to find her:** The [[Shrine of Luck]] on the south side of [[Phandalin]] — a small stone shrine with a Tymora coin medallion above the door.
- **Disposition:** Warm, bright, slightly nervous when first met. Becomes much steadier when she trusts you.
- **Currently:** Recovering from wounds. She left town a tenday ago and came back bloodied. She does not talk about it.

---

## Personality

Garaele is in her sixties (young, for an elf) and has the slightly raw energy of someone who has not yet decided what kind of person they will be. She believes — fervently and without irony — in luck. Not as chance, but as a _principle_: that the universe arranges itself in favor of those brave enough to push it.

She is friendly to strangers and quick to bless a coin or a weapon at the shrine ("for the next hard moment, may it tilt your way"). She is _not_ a pushover. When her trust is earned, she is fierce.

---

## What She Will Share

- She knows of the [[The Redbrands|Redbrands]] and despises them in the quiet, principled way a cleric of Tymora is allowed to despise anything. She has heard the name [[Glasstaff]] but does not know who he really is.
- She keeps a friendly ear out for travelers passing through [[Phandalin]] and remembers names and faces unusually well.
- She knows the banshee **[[Agatha]]** of [[Conyberry]] is dangerous, vain, and territorial — and that anyone foolish enough to seek her out should bring the right gift and the right manners.

---

## Quest: A Question for Agatha

Garaele will offer the party the chance to do something for her.

> [!readaloud]
> "There is a banshee at Conyberry. Her name is Agatha. She is old, and she is vain, and she once — _once_, long ago — answered a question for a wizard who knew how to ask. I would very much like her to answer one more."
>
> "I cannot go back myself. She… does not want to see me again. But she might see _you_. If you take her a gift — a silver comb, her hair was famous in life — and you treat her as a queen would expect to be treated, she may answer. _One_ question. No more."

**The question to ask:** _"Where is the spellbook of Bowgentle?"_ The party must commit Garaele's exact phrasing to memory.

**The gift:** A silver comb. Garaele provides one — a delicate filigree piece worth roughly 25gp.

**Reward on return:** A [[potions-of-healing|Potion of Healing]] from her shrine reserves, 25gp, and her personal thanks.
