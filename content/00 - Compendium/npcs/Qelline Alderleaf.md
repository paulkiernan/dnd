# Qelline Alderleaf — Halfling Farmer

_Wise, pragmatic halfling farmer who runs [[Alderleaf Farm]] on the southern edge of [[Phandalin]]. Mother of [[Carp Alderleaf]]. Possibly the most genuinely competent person in town._

---

## At a Glance

- **Race / Profession:** Halfling, mid-forties, farmer.
- **Where to find her:** [[Alderleaf Farm]], a tidy holding south of the town square.
- **Family:** Her son **[[Carp Alderleaf|Carp]]** (ten years old, fearless to a fault).

---

## Personality

Qelline is steady. She has lived through more than she lets on. She watched two Phandalins burn — once as a child when Lord Tresendar's manor was sacked, again as a young woman when the [[The Sword Mountains|Sword Mountains]] orcs came down on Conyberry and the survivors fled here. She has buried a husband and one child. Her remaining son [[Carp Alderleaf|Carp]] is the center of her world.

She does not panic. She does not exaggerate. When she says something is wrong, it is wrong.

---

## What She Wants

Qelline is genuinely worried about her boy.

> [!readaloud]
> "[[Carp Alderleaf|Carp]] told me he found a tunnel in the woods east of the orchard. He says he heard mean voices coming out of it. He's eight. He's going to go back there with or without me — that's the kind of boy he is. I'd very much rather he goes back with people who can keep him alive."

**The ask:** Take Carp with you, have him lead you to the tunnel, and either confirm it's nothing or confirm it's something. Either way, _do not let him go down into it._

**Reward:** Free room and board at [[Alderleaf Farm]] for as long as the party wants. A hot meal every night. Carp's hero-worship.

---

## What She Knows

- The hills and woods around [[Phandalin]] better than anyone. She can name every game trail, every standing stone, every old grave.
- The Old Owl Well shepherd who fled the "walking corpses" came to her farm for water on his way back to town. She heard his story before [[Daran Edermath|Daran]] did.
- She is offering her hayloft as a free bunkroom for the party if they need it. She is _not_ doing this out of charity — she wants competent armed people sleeping near her farm.

---
