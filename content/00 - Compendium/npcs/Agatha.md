# Agatha — The Banshee of Conyberry

_The wailing remains of an elven noblewoman who died wronged, three hundred years ago, in a forest north of what is now [[Conyberry]]._

---

## In Life

In life she was **Agatha of the Moonspun**, an elven noblewoman of some minor house out of [[Neverwinter]]. The histories give different accounts of how she died — betrayed by a lover, betrayed by a sister, betrayed by herself; the songs are inconsistent. What is agreed: she was beautiful in a way that made men do stupid things, she was vain in a way that made her enemies do worse, and she died in a clearing in the woods with a knife in her ribs and her hair shorn at the scalp.

She has not forgotten the second part.

---

## In Death

Agatha is now a **banshee**. She haunts the clearing where she died, half a mile north of the [[Conyberry]] ruins, where a grass-grown mound and a single standing stone mark her grave.

She has been there for three centuries. The few visitors who have approached her and lived describe a pale figure rising from the mound without disturbing a single blade of grass, hair the color of frost, eyes of cold white fire.
