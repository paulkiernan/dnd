# Edermath Orchard

_A tidy apple orchard on the east edge of [[Phandalin]], owned and slowly being restored by [[Daran Edermath]]._

The orchard grew wild for decades after the original Phandalin fell. Daran has been clearing the underbrush, pruning, replanting, and grafting — the kind of slow work a five-hundred-year-old retired adventurer has the patience for.

His cottage sits at the eastern edge of the orchard: a single-story stone building with a slate roof, a kitchen garden, and a small lean-to shed that contains, among other things, a hauberk that has been through more battles than most people will ever see.

The orchard supplies most of [[Phandalin]]'s cider — including the cider at the [[Stonehill Inn]].

**Resident:** [[Daran Edermath]].
