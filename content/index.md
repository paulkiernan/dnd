## Where We Are

The party arrived in [[Phandalin]], collected their pay from [[Barthen's Provisions|Barthen]] and [[Linene Graywind|Linene]], and identified the strange newt figurine from [[Cragmaw Hideout]] as a [[figurine-of-wondrous-power|Figurine of Wondrous Power]] (now named **Newton**). They then crossed paths with four drunk [[The Redbrands|Redbrands]] led by **Graul** on the steps of the [[Shrine of Luck]] — and ended the meeting with four dead Redbrands.

To keep the killings from going on the townmaster's books as murder, [[Sildar Hallwinter]] formally hired the party as [[The Lords' Alliance|Lords' Alliance]] contractors. The party was then sworn in by [[Harbin Wester|Townmaster Wester]] as **Phandalin protectorate** — with the writ to prove it. The town is now openly grateful, and openly afraid of the retaliation.

The party met [[Sister Garaele]] at the shrine and accepted her quest to put a question to the banshee [[Agatha]] at [[Conyberry]]. They also scouted both Redbrand strongholds — the [[Sleeping Giant]] (7 Redbrands plus a bartender; two exits) and the **secret tunnel** in the woods behind [[Tresendar Manor]] (2 guards posted at the cave mouth, found via [[Carp Alderleaf|Carp]]'s tip).

## Phandalin — Your Base of Operations

[[Phandalin]] is a small frontier town in the foothills of the [[The Sword Mountains|Sword Mountains]], a few days' travel south of [[Neverwinter]].

**Places in town:**
- [[Stonehill Inn]] — where you're sleeping
- [[Barthen's Provisions]] — general store; Gundren's drop-off point
- [[Lionshield Coster]] — weapons & armor
- [[Miner's Exchange]] — ore assayer
- [[Shrine of Luck]] — Tymora's shrine
- [[Townmaster's Hall]] — Phandalin's seat of government (such as it is)
- [[Edermath Orchard]] — Daran's place
- [[Alderleaf Farm]] — Qelline's farm
- [[Tresendar Manor]] — the troubled ruin on the eastern hill
- [[Sleeping Giant]] — the Redbrand tap house, also east end

**Townsfolk you've met:**
- [[Sildar Hallwinter]] — your patron, Lords' Alliance agent
- [[Toblen Stonehill]] — innkeeper at the Stonehill, friendly and informative
- [[Linene Graywind]] — Lionshield Coster, principled, owes you a debt
- [[Sister Garaele]] — cleric of [[forgotten-realms-tymora|Tymora]] at the Shrine; sent you to the banshee
- [[Harbin Wester]] — townmaster, well-meaning, ineffectual; swore you in as Phandalin protectorate
- [[Carp Alderleaf]] — 10-year-old halfling boy; tipped you a copper and led you to the secret tunnel

**Townsfolk you've heard about but not yet met:**
- [[Trilena Stonehill]] — Toblen's wife and the inn's cook, sharper than her husband
- [[Daran Edermath]] — retired adventurer at the orchard
- [[Halia Thornton]] — runs the Miner's Exchange
- [[Qelline Alderleaf]] — halfling farmer; mother of [[Carp Alderleaf|Carp]]
- [[Thel Dendrar]] — woodcarver, murdered by the Redbrands a tenday before you arrived
- [[Mirna Dendrar]] — Thel's wife, missing since the day he was killed
