# Lionshield Coster

_A trading post affiliated with the **Lionshield merchant company** out of [[Yartar]]. Run in [[Phandalin]] by [[Linene Graywind]]. The town's only source of weapons and armor in any quantity._

Located on the north side of [[Phandalin]]'s town square. A solid timber building with a lion-and-shield sign over the door. Linene keeps weapons and armor in a locked back room and brings them out for serious buyers.

The Lionshield company has been hit hard on the [[Triboar Trail]] in recent months — caravans raided, supplies stolen — much of it traceable to the [[The Redbrands|Redbrand]] gang or the goblins they pay to do the dirty work.

**Staff:** [[Linene Graywind]] (agent in charge).

**Prices:** Standard PHB rates for [[adventuring-gear-phb|adventuring gear]] under 50gp. Will _not_ sell to anyone she suspects of being a threat to the town.
