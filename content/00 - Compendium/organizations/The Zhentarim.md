# The Zhentarim

_A mercantile, militant, and not infrequently criminal network operating across [[Faerûn]]. Mercenaries, merchants, spies, and smugglers united by a single principle: **profit, on Zhent terms.**_

---

## In Brief

The Zhentarim — the "Black Network" — present themselves publicly as a respectable mercenary company that offers reliable contract security at fair prices. This is partially true. They _do_ provide reliable mercenary services. They are also widely suspected (and in some cities, well-documented) to engage in smuggling, extortion, intelligence-trading, and the quiet absorption of local crime syndicates wherever they operate.

The Network has been clawing its way back to respectability after a series of internal civil wars and external defeats. They are currently in an **expansion phase**, extending their reach into smaller frontier towns and rural regions where they can establish operations without drawing the attention of larger powers like the [[The Lords' Alliance|Lords' Alliance]] or the [[The Harpers|Harpers]].

---

## Reputation Across the Sword Coast

- In [[Neverwinter]]: tolerated, distrusted. The city's authorities watch them closely.
- In [[Waterdeep]]: present but quiet — the city's masked Lords do not allow open Zhent operations.
- In smaller frontier towns: increasingly visible. Zhent agents often pose as ordinary merchants, lenders, or assayers.

---

## Connections

- **Rivals:** [[The Harpers]] (the Network's natural enemy), [[The Lords' Alliance]] (regulatory headache)
- **Operating principle:** Profit. Always profit. Methods negotiable.
