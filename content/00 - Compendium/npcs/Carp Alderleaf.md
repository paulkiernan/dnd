# Carp Alderleaf

_Ten-year-old halfling boy. Son of [[Qelline Alderleaf]]. Lives at [[Alderleaf Farm]]. Fearless to a fault. Best friend of **Pip Stonehill**, the innkeeper's son._

---

## What He's Been Up To

Carp has — by his own breathless account — found a **secret tunnel** in the woods east of the orchard, behind a tangle of brambles and a boulder. He went in ten steps before a rat scared him, but he says he heard adult voices coming from deeper in. _Mean_ voices, he insists.

He has not told anyone but his mother and Pip. He has been pestering both of them to let him go back and explore further. His mother has very firmly told him no.
