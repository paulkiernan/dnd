# Tresendar Manor — The Ruin on the Hill

_The crumbling stone manor on the wooded hill at the east edge of [[Phandalin]]. Long abandoned. Currently associated, in town gossip, with the [[The Redbrands|Redbrands]]._

---

## What's Known

Tresendar Manor was the seat of a minor noble family of the same name, abandoned for the better part of two centuries. Above ground, it is little more than crumbling walls, a collapsed second story, and a weed-eaten foundation. The original chapel still stands — barely — with a broken stained-glass window and the bones of a wooden pew or two.

The cellars are said to run deep. Local folklore claims the Tresendars built them as a defensive last redoubt, and that they include an old family crypt. Nobody alive in Phandalin has been down there.

---

## Why People Stay Away

Two reasons:

1. **Superstition.** Locals have always given the ruin a wide berth — there are old stories of strange noises and ill-luck for anyone who lingered there.
2. **The Redbrands.** Several townsfolk have noticed [[The Redbrands|Redbrand]] thugs coming and going from the manor at odd hours. Whatever the gang is using it for, it isn't pleasant, and approaching the place is a good way to attract a scarlet-cloaked welcoming committee.

---

## Approach

The path up the hill is overlooked from the manor itself — there is no way to walk up the front approach without being seen from the chapel windows. The manor sits on a low rise of grey stone ringed by old trees. The front gates are gone; only the hinges remain.

The east wing has collapsed entirely; vines have taken what's left.
