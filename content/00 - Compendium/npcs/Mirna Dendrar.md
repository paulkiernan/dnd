# Mirna Dendrar

_Widow of the murdered [[Thel Dendrar]]. Mother of two — **Nars** (12) and **Nilsa** (18). Missing since the day of her husband's murder._

---

## What's Known

Mirna was a thoughtful, quiet woman who helped run her husband's woodcarving shop on the south side of [[Phandalin]]'s town square. She kept the books, handled the customers, and was known for the small spice garden she tended beside the shop.

On the day [[Thel Dendrar|Thel]] was killed by the [[The Redbrands|Redbrands]] in the street, several townsfolk saw Redbrand thugs return to the shop within an hour. Mirna and the two children have not been seen since. The shop has been shuttered ever since.

The townsfolk fear the worst. **Trilena Stonehill**, who was a friend, leaves flowers at Thel's unmarked grave-stone every tenday. She refuses to speak of Mirna in the past tense and will not let the conversation turn that way.
