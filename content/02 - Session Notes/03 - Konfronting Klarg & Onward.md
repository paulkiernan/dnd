## The Story So Far

The party finished the [[Cragmaw Hideout]] crawl by defeating the bugbear chief **Klarg** in his den. They rescued [[Sildar Hallwinter]], recovered the [[Lionshield Coster|Lionshield]] caravan goods, and a strange figurine identified by one of the druids as a [[figurine-of-wondrous-power|Figurine of Wondrous Power]] in the shape of a newt.

The party then made their way back to [[Phandalin]] along the [[Triboar Trail]] with Sildar and the recovered cargo. They were paid by [[Barthen's Provisions|Elmar Barthen]] for delivering [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]]'s supplies, and rewarded by [[Linene Graywind]] of the [[Lionshield Coster]] for recovering the stolen cargo.

That evening, while exploring [[Phandalin]] for the first time, the party crossed paths with a group of [[The Redbrands|Redbrands]] in the town square who attempted to collect a "newcomer tax" — and the encounter went hot.

---

## Loose Threads

- [[Gundren Rockseeker]] is being held at a place called [[Cragmaw Castle]], somewhere in the [[Neverwinter Wood]]. [[Sildar Hallwinter]] is trying to learn its location.
- A wizard named [[Iarno Albrek]] has been missing in [[Phandalin]] for nearly a year. Sildar would like him found.
- The [[The Redbrands|Redbrands]]' presence in [[Phandalin]] is no longer a rumor — it is a problem.
