# Session 3: Crawl in the Cave

**Played:** (Session 3 — at Timber Cove)
**Setting:** [[Cragmaw Hideout]], a cave system in the hills off the [[Triboar Trail]]

---

## Recap

The party pressed deeper into the goblin cave they had broken into at the end of the previous session.

### The Kennel (and the Wolves)

At the cave entrance area, the party found a kennel where the goblins kept three [[wolf|wolves]] chained against the wall. Rather than fight, the party negotiated — feeding the wolves enough scraps to win their tolerance, then breaking the chains. The wolves followed the party for the rest of the session as loose allies.

### Yeemik and the Hostage

A short climb led to the **goblin den (H6)**, a large open chamber with a rocky shelf at the top. On the shelf, **Yeemik** — a goblin boss — held [[Sildar Hallwinter]] hostage at knifepoint. Sildar was bloody, stripped of his armor and weapons, weak to the point of collapse.

Yeemik tried to negotiate: the party would kill **Klarg** (the cave's bugbear chief) deeper in the hideout in exchange for Sildar's life. Sildar himself muttered, even half-dead, that the party should not trust the goblin.

The party didn't. The negotiation broke down and **Yeemik shoved Sildar over the edge** before the fight began — Sildar dropped to 0 HP from the fall but was stabilized by the party during the combat. Yeemik and the rest of his goblins were killed. The captured goblin from the previous session, **Peetrr**, witnessed the killing of his friends one too many times and fled — only to be killed by the wolves at the bottom of the cliff.

After the fight, Grom noticed something on Sildar's chest, just above his heart: the tattoo of **[[The Griffon Cavalry of Waterdeep|the Griffon Cavalry of Waterdeep]]**. A small, very particular mark. Grom recognized it.

The party recovered an **ornate feather** from Yeemik's body — one for each druid in the party.

### The Twin Pools and the Smoke Trap

The party reached the **Twin Pools Cave (H7)**, a cavern half-filled with water and a small waterfall feeding it. Above, somewhere deeper in the cave system, was Klarg's throne room.

Rather than push straight in, the party set up a trap. Orla lit a fire at the base of a natural chimney in the wolf den below, and the smoke curled up and filled the chamber above. Klarg sent his guards to investigate.

The party ambushed them on the way: **2 goblins and a wolf**, dispatched cleanly.

### Klarg's Door

With Klarg's guards dead, the party advanced on the rear chamber. **Orla cast [[faerie-fire|Faerie Fire]]** through the door, lighting the cavern in pale violet — and revealing Klarg himself, his throne piled high with stolen crates and goods, still smoke-blind and groping for his weapon.

**Initiative was rolled. Cliffhanger.**

## What the Party Walks Away With

- [[Sildar Hallwinter|Sildar]] is **alive** (barely) and free
- Sildar wears the tattoo of the [[The Griffon Cavalry of Waterdeep|Griffon Cavalry of Waterdeep]] — Grom knows what this means
- Three **wolves** following the party as loose allies
- An **ornate feather** for each druid (Yeemik's leavings)
- Klarg is one room away, blind in the smoke
