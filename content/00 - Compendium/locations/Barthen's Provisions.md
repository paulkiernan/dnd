# Barthen's Provisions

_The largest general store in [[Phandalin]]. Owned and run by **Elmar Barthen**, a lean, balding human with ink-stained sleeves and a friendly demeanor._

Located on the north side of [[Phandalin]]'s town square, Barthen's stocks everything a settler, miner, or adventurer might need _short of_ weapons and armor (for that, see the [[Lionshield Coster]]): rope, rations, mining tools, torches, oil, lanterns, bedrolls, ink, paper, simple cookware, dried herbs, salt, candles, and a hundred other small things.

Elmar runs the shop with a couple of young clerks. He is well-connected with the merchant caravans running between [[Neverwinter]] and [[Phandalin]] and is generally the first person to hear of road trouble. He has been "taxed" by [[The Redbrands|Redbrand]] thugs more than once and is quietly furious about it.

The party delivered [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]]'s wagonload of provisions here. Elmar paid them **10gp each** as promised and threw in extra rations and basic supplies on the house.

**Prices:** Standard PHB rates for non-weapon [[adventuring-gear-phb|adventuring gear]] under 25gp.

**Proprietor:** Elmar Barthen.
