# The Order of the Gauntlet

_A militant order of faithful seekers of justice. Members swear to protect the innocent, smite evil, and bring retribution against those who would prey on others._

---

## In Brief

The Order of the Gauntlet draws its members from clerics, paladins, monks, and dedicated lay warriors — people of faith who believe that **virtue requires action**, not just contemplation. Members come from many different deities (Tyr, Torm, Helm, Bahamut, and others), unified by a shared commitment to active justice.

The Order is well-organized, well-funded, and unafraid to act publicly. They run training houses, sponsor temple militias, and quietly bankroll adventuring parties whose interests align with their own.

---

## Local Member

**[[Daran Edermath]]** — retired drow marshal of the Dragon Coast, now of [[Phandalin]]. He keeps an orchard and an ear to the ground. The Order considers him "honorably retired" but he is, in practice, their eyes in the western Sword Coast frontier.

---

## What The Order Wants In Phandalin

- The [[The Redbrands|Redbrands]] gone — by force if necessary, by trial if possible.
- Any servants of greater evils (cultists, undead, aberrations) identified and removed.
- Reliable adventurers known _by name_ to the Order, who can be called on for future missions.
