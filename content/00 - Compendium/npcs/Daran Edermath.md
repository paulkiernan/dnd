# Daran Edermath — The Orchard-Keeper

_Retired drow adventurer. Member of the [[The Order of the Gauntlet|Order of the Gauntlet]]. Lives quietly in a cottage beside the apple orchard at the eastern edge of [[Phandalin]]._

---

## At a Glance

- **Race / Profession:** Drow (rare in this region — locals don't comment on it because Daran has earned his place); former marshal and herald of the Dragon Coast.
- **Age:** Well over 500 years old. He carries himself like it.
- **Where to find him:** [[Edermath Orchard]] on the east edge of town, or — when in town — at the [[Stonehill Inn]] nursing a single cup of cider.
- **Disposition:** Quiet, watchful, slow to speak. Warms quickly to anyone who shares his sense of justice.

---

## Personality

Daran speaks in short, measured sentences. He listens twice as long as he speaks. He is the kind of man who notices the corner of a room first and the people in it second. He has lived long enough to recognize courage and stupidity at a glance — and to find both endearing in moderation.

If asked about his past, he will tell the truth in the fewest words possible. He served as a marshal and herald in the lands of the Dragon Coast, far to the south, for "longer than I will say." He retired to the Neverwinter region — his original home — to grow apples and let his weapons rust.

He has not let his weapons rust.

---

## What He Will Share

Daran has been quietly keeping eyes on [[Phandalin]] since the [[The Redbrands|Redbrands]] arrived. He will tell anyone he judges competent and well-intentioned:

- The [[The Redbrands|Redbrands]] are not a mercenary guild. They are a gang, organized by someone who knows what they are doing.
- Their unofficial headquarters is the [[Sleeping Giant]] tap house. He has seen as many as eight Redbrands there at one time.
- Their _actual_ stronghold appears to be beneath [[Tresendar Manor]]. He has watched them come and go from the ruins at night.
- Their leader is called **[[Glasstaff]]**, but he has never seen the man face-to-face.
- He has heard whispers of weird-looking goblins moving in the hills lately — bigger eyes, paler skin. He doesn't like it.

---

## Quest: Old Owl Trouble

**The ask:** Daran wants the party to ride out to **[[Old Owl Well]]**, the ruined Netherese watchtower in the hills northeast of Phandalin, and find out what's making camp there. A shepherd he trusts saw "walking corpses" near the tower a tenday ago and barely got away. Daran is too old to make the trip himself.

**Reward:** 100gp on completion, plus a letter of introduction to the [[The Order of the Gauntlet|Order of the Gauntlet]].

**Secondary ask:** If the party is willing, he wants them to deal with the [[The Redbrands|Redbrands]] too. He will not pay them for this — he considers it a moral imperative, not a contract — but he will help with information and, if it comes to it, with a sword at their back.
