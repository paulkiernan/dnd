# Iarno Albrek

_A wizard of the [[The Lords' Alliance|Lords' Alliance]] who was sent to [[Phandalin]] roughly a year ago to help bring order to the frontier town. He has not been heard from since shortly after his arrival._

---

## What's Known

[[Sildar Hallwinter]] is looking for him. Sildar describes Iarno as:

> "Short. Dark-bearded. Maybe thirty-five. A real wizard — not a hedge-trick artist, the genuine article. He came south for the Alliance about a year ago. Said he was going to put eyes on Phandalin and make sure the town didn't fall apart. We haven't heard a word from him since the second tenday after he arrived."

Sildar fears Iarno may be dead, or held prisoner by the same gang ([[The Redbrands]]) currently terrorizing the town. He is offering **50gp** to anyone who can find him — alive if possible, the truth either way.

The few Phandalin townsfolk who remember Iarno describe him as quiet, businesslike, and curious about the town's records. [[Harbin Wester]] (the townmaster) recalls Iarno asking about Phandalin's tax rolls in their one meeting, and assumed he had simply returned to [[Neverwinter]] when no follow-up came.
