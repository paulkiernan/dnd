# Yartar

_A medium-sized trading city on the **Dessarin River** in the **Dessarin Valley**, east of [[Waterdeep]] and northeast of [[The High Road]]. Best known to this campaign as the home office of the **Lionshield merchant company**._

Yartar is a river city that sits at the intersection of several trade routes between the [[The Sword Coast|Sword Coast]] and the interior. Governed by a "Waterbaron" (an elected magistrate with naval and trade authority), it is a wealthy, mercantile, slightly seedy town with a strong merchant culture and a quietly fearsome river-watch.

**Why it comes up in this campaign:** [[Linene Graywind]] runs the local [[Lionshield Coster]] post as an agent of the Yartar-based Lionshield merchant company. Caravans bound for Phandalin originate, in part, from Yartar. If the party makes a strong impression on Linene, she will offer them a letter of recommendation to the Lionshield home office in Yartar — useful for future arcs in the Dessarin Valley.
