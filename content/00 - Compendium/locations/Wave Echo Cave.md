# Wave Echo Cave

_The legendary lost dwarven mine of the **Phandelver's Pact**. Hidden in the hills somewhere northeast of [[Phandalin]]. Lost for five centuries._

---

## The Legend

Five hundred years ago, a coalition of dwarves and gnomes of the **Phandelver's Pact** mined a rich vein of magical mithral and other rare ores in a cave system in the hills near what was then [[Phandalin]]. Deep within the cave, at the source of the magic, they built the **Forge of Spells** — an enchanted forge capable of imbuing weapons, armor, and wondrous items with permanent magical properties.

For a generation, the Pact prospered and Phandalin thrived. Then, in a single brutal week:
- Orcs swept south from the [[The Sword Mountains|Sword Mountains]] and destroyed Phandalin.
- A force of *something else* — accounts disagree; the surviving songs say "creatures of the deep dark" — struck the Forge of Spells itself, killing every dwarf and gnome inside.
- The entrance to the cave collapsed.

The cave has been **lost** ever since. Generations of prospectors have searched. None have found it. Until — perhaps — now.

---

## What [[Gundren Rockseeker]] Found

[[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]] and his brothers **Tharden** and **Nundro** spent years following old family records and survey notes, and recently believe they have located the entrance. Gundren was riding to [[Phandalin]] to begin operations when he was ambushed and captured. The **map to the cave** was on his person.

His brothers Tharden and Nundro are reportedly camped near the cave entrance, waiting for Gundren. They do not yet know what has become of him.

---

## Why It Matters

The Forge of Spells, if it still works, would be a standing magical capability — the kind of thing that has not existed on the western [[The Sword Coast|Sword Coast]] for generations. Whoever controls it controls the ability to craft real magical items. That alone is worth a great deal of trouble.
