# The Miner's Exchange

_The trading post where local miners bring ore to be weighed, assayed, and sold. Run by [[Halia Thornton]]. Quietly: the [[The Zhentarim|Zhentarim]] front in [[Phandalin]]._

A solid two-story building on the south side of [[Phandalin]]'s town square. Brass scales, locked strongboxes, a long counter with iron grilles. The back room is Halia's office — wood-paneled, with a writing desk, a wine cabinet, and a second door out the back.

The Exchange handles **all** ore traffic in the region. Halia keeps careful records of who is finding what, and where — information that is, by itself, worth a small fortune to the right buyer.

The [[The Redbrands|Redbrands]] do not hassle the Miner's Exchange. The reason is not advertised.

**Resident / proprietor:** [[Halia Thornton]].
