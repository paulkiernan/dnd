# Alderleaf Farm

_A modest, well-kept halfling farmstead on the southern edge of [[Phandalin]]. Run by [[Qelline Alderleaf]] and her son [[Carp Alderleaf|Carp]]._

A small cluster of buildings: a stone-and-timber farmhouse with a low door (built for halflings; humans must duck), a tidy barn, a chicken coop, a vegetable garden, and an apple-tree-shaded yard where Carp plays.

The farm produces vegetables, eggs, and a single milk cow's worth of butter — most of which goes to the [[Stonehill Inn]] and the [[Lionshield Coster]] as part of standing trade agreements.

[[Qelline Alderleaf|Qelline]] has offered the use of her **hayloft** as a free bunkroom for the party if they need a quiet place to sleep that isn't the inn. It is a genuinely good offer.

**Residents:** [[Qelline Alderleaf]], [[Carp Alderleaf]].
